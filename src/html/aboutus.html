About Us

In our fourth year of studies, we are an enthusiastic and dedicated team that has come together to develop "CashMe," an innovative personal finance app. Our goal is to gamify financial management, making money handling more accessible and enjoyable for everyone. We feel highly motivated by the possibility of empowering users on their journey toward financial health, providing tools to help them save, spend wisely, and achieve their financial goals. We believe that by transforming how people interact with their finances, we can make a significant difference in their lives.
